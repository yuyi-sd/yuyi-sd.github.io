About me

Welcome! I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow working with Prof. Xudong Jiang at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. I received my Ph.D. from Nanyang Technological University (NTU) in 2025, advised by Prof. Alex C. Kot, Prof. Yap-Peng Tan, and Prof. Shijian Lu. Earlier, I earned an M.S. in Machine Learning & Data Science from the University of California, San Diego (2020) and a B.E. in Automation from Tsinghua University (2019).

My research focuses on Trustworthy Machine Learning and AI Security, aiming to enhance the security and privacy of AI methods across their full life cycle. I have worked extensively on adversarial attacks, backdoor attacks, and data poisoning (unlearnable examples) as well as their mitigations. Recently, my interests extend to data privacy (e.g., protecting data from unauthorized commercial training and model inversion risks) and the security of foundation models such as LLMs and multimodal systems.
